Shaded in Silence

Four weeks before harvest, tea fields are covered in dark screens, forcing the leaves to produce intense chlorophyll and rich L-theanine for a sweet, deep umami taste.

Hand-Selected Tencha

Only the tenderest top leaves are hand-picked by masters in early spring. The leaves are quickly steamed, air-dried, and sorted to extract pure "Tencha."

Slow Stone Grinding

Traditional granite mills turn the leaves into fine powder. To prevent friction heat from scorching the delicate aroma, a mill grinds just 30 grams per hour.

The Ultimate Whisk

Add hot water, grab the Chasen bamboo whisk, and stir vigorously in a W-pattern until a creamy jade froth crowns your bowl. A ritual of clarity.
